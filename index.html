Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? Our FAQs might have the answer! Please check them before contacting support.

1 How to place an order with RootRise Glabal?

For easy ordering, visit us on Amazon.in or Flipkart.com. Alternatively, you can place your order directly via WhatsApp at 6239983870.

2 Can I change or modify orders that are already placed?

You would not be able to change or modify the number of items once you have already placed an order. You can place another fresh order with the desired modifications.

3 Can I cancel the order once it is placed?

Orders may be cancelled post-placement, with the exception of food items, which are final and non-cancellable due to safety and hygiene protocols.

1 Is pricing negotiable for distributors or partners?

Yes, we welcome partnerships! Pricing is flexible for long-term collaborators. Let’s talk.

2 Do bulk orders receive a discount?

Yes! We offer volume-based pricing for bulk exports. Please contact our sales team for customized quotes.

3 Do you offer subscription or recurring order discounts?

For recurring shipments, we offer loyalty perks and stable pricing. Reach out for tailored plans.

1 How can I reach your support team?

You can contact us via WhatsApp at +91 6239983870 or email us directly at support@rootriseglobal.com. We aim to respond within 24–48 hours.

2 What if I face an issue with my order?

Please share your order ID and details of the issue, and our team will work swiftly to resolve it—whether it’s a shipment delay or product concern.

3 Is support available for international customers?

Absolutely. Our support team assists global buyers with queries related to exports, customs, and documentation.
